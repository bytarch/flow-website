About Flow

Learn about our mission and the technology behind Flow

Our Origin

Flow is a closed-source application that originated as a fork of the innovative open-source tool called Dyad. We want to express our sincere appreciation to the Dyad project for making Flow possible.

Flow is not a 1-1 copy of Dyad. Internally, Flow has been significantly modified to work with our proprietary API and partnered service Neon NextGeneration, which makes everything possible on Flow.

Why Closed Source?

Flow is closed source because it has been extensively modified and includes deep integration with our primary services. These integrations are what enable the unique capabilities and seamless experience that Flow provides.

Our proprietary technology and partnerships allow us to offer a level of service and functionality that wouldn't be possible in a fully open-source model.

What We Do

Flow generates production-ready front-end applications based on your descriptions. Simply tell us what you want to build, and our AI will create the code for you.

Currently, Flow supports Next.js (static), Vite.js, and HTML (beta). All generated code follows best practices and is ready for deployment. Flow is continuously evolving to better your developer experience.

Our Community

Flow is built by developers, for developers. We're committed to creating a tool that transforms software development and makes it accessible to everyone.

We welcome feedback and ideas from our users as we continue to improve Flow and expand its capabilities.

Our Values

Quality
